With the help of countless cups of coffee, I was able graduate with the highest honors (or honours if you want to spell it incorrectly...) in December of 2018 all while taking courses such as Distributed AI (Multi-agent Reinforcement Learning), Bayesian Statistics, Likelihood Inference, and Data Mining (Neural Networks).

Soon after I graduated from my Masters program, I then decided to make another leap and moved to Cologne, Germany, which is where I started my career as a Data Scientist for an Artificial Intelligence start-up called Fedger.io. At this company, I was responsible for implementing from scratch (using Keras and Tensorflow) our deep learning object detection architecture and stayed up-to-date with the most recent object detection research to see what can additionally be implemented to increase our metrics.

After a little over a year at Fedger.io, I then decided to join the leading data science and AI consultancy in Germany, Alexander Thamm GmbH, as a Junior Machine Learning Engineer in January 2020, which is where I currently am right now!
